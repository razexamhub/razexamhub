About Raz Exam Hub – Your Trusted Exam Center

Raz Exam Hub is an independent, secure testing facility based in Kochi, Kerala.

We are committed to providing a professional and reliable environment for candidates taking internationally recognized certification exams. As a potential Prometric-authorized test center, our focus is on delivering computer-based testing solutions that meet global standards of security, integrity, and convenience.

Our facility is designed to support exams across domains such as finance, healthcare, IT, and language proficiency, including exams like CMA (USA), GRE, and others.

We aim to create a smooth, stress-free testing experience — ensuring every candidate can focus fully on their exam in a quiet and well-managed space.
